03 · Clearspace & minimum size

Breathing room

  • Clearspace — always reserve a margin equal to the pin length (x = 10u at 64u artboard, ~16% of the mark) on every side.
  • Minimum horizontal lockup — 120px wide. Below that, drop the wordmark and use the mark only.
  • Minimum mark (favicon) — 16px with the pinout simplified (pins become single pixels, braces thin to 0.9u stroke).
  • Alignment — optical centre sits on the chip body's centre, not the SVG viewbox centre. When using the mark next to another logo, align to the body, not the pins.
